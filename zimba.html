Building Brand of Zimba

Project information

  • Category: Graphic Art
  • Client: Annapurna Swadisht
  • Brand: Zimba

Traffic Grid Advertising successfully launched and promoted Annapurna Swadisht's snack brand, Zimba. We created a vibrant brand identity, implemented a dynamic social media strategy, and collaborated with influencers to boost engagement. Our efforts quickly established Zimba as a beloved name in the snack industry, increasing online presence and sales, and fostering a loyal community of enthusiasts.
