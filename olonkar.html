Package Design for Olonkar

Project information

  • Category: Package Design
  • Client: Annapurna Swadisht

For Annapurna Swadisht's brand Olonkar, Traffic Grid Advertising crafted eye-catching packaging that stands out on shelves and resonates with the brand’s vibrant identity. Each design combines bold colors, playful graphics, and clear product information, making it visually appealing and easily recognizable. Our packaging not only attracts attention but also conveys the quality and deliciousness of the products inside, enhancing the overall customer experience.
